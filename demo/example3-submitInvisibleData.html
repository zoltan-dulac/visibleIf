This two forms in this example are identical, except the first is a member of the visibleIf-submitInvisibleData class, and the second isn't. Try submitting both forms without filling out any of the fields. You will notice the first one submits only the visible fields, while the second submits the visible ones and the address field, no matter if it's visible or not (due to the address field being a member of the visibleIf-submitIfInvisible class).
